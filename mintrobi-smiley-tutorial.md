# mintrobi-smiley-tutorial
## Step 1

Add the Show LEDs block to the ```on start``` block.
```blocks
basic.showLeds(`
    . . . . .
    . . . . .
    . . . . .
    . . . . .
    . . . . .
    `)
```
## Step 2
Then draw a smiley:
```blocks
basic.showLeds(`
    . . . . .
    . # . # .
    . . . . .
    # . . . #
    . # # # .
    `)
```
